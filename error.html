Services

Services

Advertisement Tax

Advertisement tax is a tax levied on businesses for advertising their products or services within the municipality's jurisdiction.

Renewal of Trade License

Renewal of trade license allows businesses to continue operating legally within the municipality by renewing their trade license annually.

Sanitation

Sanitation services include waste collection, disposal, and recycling to maintain cleanliness and hygiene within the municipality.

Building Permissions

Building permissions are required for any construction or renovation projects within the municipality's jurisdiction to ensure compliance with zoning regulations and safety standards.

Pay Property Tax Online

Pay property tax online allows property owners to conveniently pay their property taxes through the municipality's website or online portal.
