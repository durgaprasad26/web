About Us

Suryapet Council

Suryapet County is a District in the State of Telangana, India. Most of the Suryapet city area is located within the District of Suryapet council. The Council of Suryapet stretches from Broken Bay to the north, the Hawkesbury River to the north-west, the Nepean River to the west, the Cataract River to the south-west and the northern suburbs of Wollongong to the south. It includes the area of the Suryapet Plain.
